Where the Wild Beers Are is an annual event that was started for beer lovers that don't mind some sour flavors. This is the ninth annual gathering of beer fans in Saint Paul, MN to celebrate the funky beers. Each year we come together to celebrate and drink wild, sour, and farmhouse brews. In the age of pure yeast brewing, wild and sour ales are making a comeback because of the fans that enjoy all flavors.
